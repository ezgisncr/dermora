Sunscreen SPF 50+

Sun protection is one of the most important steps for skin health not only during summer but all year long. La Roche-Posay's Anthelios UVMune 400 Invisible Fluid SPF50+ offers cutting-edge UV protection technology.

This product, enriched with Mexoryl 400 filter, provides superior protection against long-wave UVA rays (380–400 nm) — one of the main causes of skin aging and DNA damage.

Advantages:

  • Ultra-light texture: Does not feel heavy or greasy; ideal for daily use.
  • Eye area safe: Non-irritating around the eyes, suitable even for sensitive areas.
  • Water, sweat, and sand-resistant: High-performance protection for outdoor activities.
  • Dermatologically tested: Suitable for all skin types, including sensitive skin.

How to Use:
Apply generously 20 minutes before sun exposure. Reapply especially after swimming, sweating, or towel drying.

Best For:
- Sensitive skin
- Hyperpigmentation concerns
- Preventing photoaging
- Urban daily protection

Effective sun protection not only prevents sunburns but also delays wrinkles, loss of elasticity, and dark spots over the long term.

Gentle Face Cleanser

For oily and acne-prone skin, balancing sebum and keeping pores clean is critical. Bioderma Sébium Gel Moussant is designed for daily cleansing of combination, oily, and acne-prone skin.

Product Features:

  • Sebum regulation: Thanks to its Fluidactiv™ complex, it helps improve sebum quality and prevents clogged pores.
  • Gentle cleansing: Soap-free formula that cleans without drying or disrupting the skin barrier.
  • Pore purifying effect: Effectively removes excess sebum and dirt, reducing acne and blackheads.
  • Physiological pH: Respects the natural balance of the skin.

Key Ingredients:
- Zinc Sulfate and Copper Sulfate (antibacterial, sebum balancing)
- Fluidactiv™ Technology (sebum quality improvement)

How to Use:
Apply to wet skin morning and night. Gently lather and rinse thoroughly with water.

Best For:
- Combination and oily skin
- Acne-prone skin
- Pore-tightening and mattifying needs

Moisturizing Cream

Dryness, tightness, and flakiness are often linked to a weakened skin barrier. CeraVe Moisturizing Cream supports the skin's natural barrier with ceramides and hyaluronic acid.

Product Features:

  • Ceramide complex: Contains three essential ceramides (1, 3, and 6-II) to restore the skin barrier.
  • Hyaluronic acid support: Enhances the skin’s moisture retention.
  • MVE® Technology: Provides 24-hour controlled hydration release.
  • Fragrance-free and non-comedogenic: Suitable even for sensitive skin.

Key Ingredients:
- Ceramides (restore skin's lipid barrier)
- Hyaluronic Acid (lock in moisture)
- Glycerin (prolong hydration)

How to Use:
Apply as needed to clean skin on face, body, and hands. Especially recommended after bathing and frequent hand washing.

Best For:
- Dry to very dry skin
- Eczema-prone skin
- Need for intense moisturization
- Sensitive skin users
